About Us

Coffeeland Football Club was established in 2010 in Chikmagalur with a mission to bring football to new heights. We aim to be the best on the pitch, with structured emphasis on grassroots, community programs, and corporate social responsibility.

We are the only club in the district to hold the record of being seven-time champions in the Dasara Cup (2014–2018, 2023).

  • Largest team in the district
  • 14 years of community impact
  • Grassroots & youth development focus

Youth Development Program

Coffeeland FC Academy was established in 2017 and is dedicated to fostering young talent, providing a pathway to professional football.

We are committed to nurturing talent and investing in a full-fledged football development program.

One of our trainees has been selected for the prestigious BFC Soccer Schools, and numerous academy players are actively participating in the KSFA Youth League.
