Club Identity

Built in the hills. Proven on the pitch.

Coffeeland Football Club was established in 2010 with a mission to raise football standards in Chikmagalur through senior football, grassroots development, community programs, and structured pathways.

We are the only club in the district to hold the record of being seven-time champions in the Dasara Cup, with a culture shaped by consistency, discipline, and local pride.

  • Largest team in the district
  • 14 years of community impact
  • Grassroots and youth development focus

Youth Development

A clear academy path from first touch to first team.

Coffeeland FC Academy was established in 2017 to foster young talent and provide a pathway toward higher-level football.

One of our trainees has been selected for the prestigious BFC Soccer Schools, and numerous academy players are actively participating in the KSFA Youth League.

Each team operates under the supervision of the Head of Youth Development, creating a consistent, high-quality training environment.

External exposure for players ready to test themselves.

Tournament football is played every weekend, with 100+ students participating weekly and 200+ across the tournament. Age groups include U8, U10, U12, and U15, with 20+ matches per season.

Special Projects

Partnerships that widen the field.

Play 365 & Juno Sports

Strategic partnership with a B-Division club aimed at enhancing visibility and sharing best practices. International scouts periodically visit the CFC Academy to identify and nurture potential talent.

Collaboration to cultivate football talent among students through professional coaching, strong facilities, mentorship, and participation in inter-school and district-level competitions.
